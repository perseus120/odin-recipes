Hamburger Recipe

Description!

Yummy. Tasty in your tounge, yummy and mouth wattering, and super simple to make!

Ingreidients

  • Meat Patty
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • tomato
  • any sause
  1. Heat up that meat on a stove till its crispy and black
  2. Place down the bottom bread
  3. place down lettuce on top of bread
  4. place meat patty on top of lettuce
  5. place tomato/toppings on meat patty
  6. Add any sause
  7. Cover with top bread
